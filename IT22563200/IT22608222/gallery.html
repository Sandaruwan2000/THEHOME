Hear are some previous projects......

Sethsiripaya Stage III
Sethsiripaya Stage III is a 25-storeyed modern office building, with floor area of over 1.3 million square feet – the largest office building in Sri Lanka. The third phase of expansion for Sri Lanka public administration hub in Battaramulla, the new complex will bring public corporations, ministries, departments, and government offices that were scattered around the city to a centralised location.
Iconic Galaxy
An initiative of the Apurva Natvar Parikh Group (ANPG) designed by award-winning architects, Iconic Galaxy is crafted to be a landmark. It has already been recognised in the residential high-rise development category at the Asia Pacific Property Awards 2020-21. Located in Rajagiriya, one of Colombo’s most sought-after suburbs, the 33-storey residential tower would be an invaluable addition to the city skyline.
Urban Regeneration
An initiative of the Urban Development Authority (UDA) of Sri Lanka, this 11-storey housing complex situated on Colombage Mawatha, Narahenpita is part of the Government’s long-term Urban Regeneration Project. The Urban Regeneration Project focuses on enhancing the status and livelihoods of under-privileged communities in urban areas around Sri Lanka’s capital.
Fairway Urban Homes
Fairway Urban Homes is a 19-storey apartment complex with 251 luxury apartments and 5 penthouses. Located in Koswatte in close proximity to Kotte, Rajagiriya, Battaramulla, and Colombo, it consists a total floor area of 40,831 sqm. The amenities of the apartment complex include a swimming pool, functions hall, fully equipped gym, activity room, and games room. The property houses a sewage a treatment plant with recycled outflow.
Prime Grand
The project involves constructing a 39 storey Super Luxury Apartment Complex comprising 319 apartments. Upon completion, the total floor area of this complex is 77,750 sqm. Located in the upscale neighbourhood of Ward Place, Colombo 07, this self-contained lifestyle complex features a 71-meter infinity pool on the 31st floor along with a pool bar, restaurant and an exclusive entertainment area.
Malapalla Complex
The project is a component of the government’s Railway Efficiency Improvement Project (REIP) and Colombo Suburban Railway Project (CSRP) implemented by the Ministry of Transport and funded by the Asian Development Bank (ADB). The project involves construction of a medium-rise 10-storey housing complex at Malapalla with 120 apartments and necessary amenities, covering a total floor area of around 125,000 square feet.
